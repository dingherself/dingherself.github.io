# Public writing

### “Putting Gender Back into Transgender Equality: On <em>Iglesias v. Federal Bureau of Prisons</em>” <span style="font-weight:500;">(<em>APA Blog</em>, Law and Philosophy Series, August 2023)</span>

Focusing on the law’s systematic failure to protect incarcerated trans women’s life, health, and bodily integrity, I critique what I see as a gender-neutral, cis-centric conception of gender equality that now dominates U.S. law.
