# Cookie Policy

*Updated on June 3, 2019.*

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of information that are placed on your computer when you visit websites. They are supposed to improve your experience using those sites---unfortunately, this has not always been the case. This Cookie Policy explains, for this particular site, what information they gather and that information is used.

You can learn more about cookies [here](https://www.cookiesandyou.com/about-cookies/).

## How does this site use cookies?

This site uses *only* Google Analytics cookies. Google Analytics is one of the most widely used and trusted analytics services on the web that reports how you use the site. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content.

Since Google Analytics cookies have separate and independent privacy policies, this site can take no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of this third party service. For more information on Google Analytics cookies, please visit [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com).

## Disabling Cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser (see your browser Help for how to do this). Please visit [this site](https://www.cookiesandyou.com/disable-cookies/) to find out how.
