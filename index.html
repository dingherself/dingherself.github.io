Welcome!

I’m a graduate student in philosophy at the University of Arizona. I work primarily on issues at the intersection of political philosophy, feminist philosophy, metaphysics, and philosophy of law.

My current research considers the ways in which metaphysical questions about social categories and political questions about equality shed novel light on each other. I’ve been particularly interested in developing a realist account of gender that speaks to the deep authenticity of trans/nonbinary people’s lived experiences and exploring its implications for how we ought to live together.

A Note on My Name

I do not use my legal last name for family-related reasons. “Ding” can function as both a first name and a last name.
