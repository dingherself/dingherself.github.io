Welcome!

I’m a PhD student in philosophy at the University of Arizona. I work primarily on issues at the intersection of feminist political philosophy, social metaphysics, and philosophy of law.

My current research considers the underappreciated ways in which transgender equality not only poses pressing challenges to, but sheds novel light on, the meaning of gender equality. My dissertation develops a trans feminist account of gender equality that takes seriously both the gender (the social metaphysics) and the equality (the political philosophy), not just one or the other.

I’m apparently also on Twitter (@dingherself), for some reason.
